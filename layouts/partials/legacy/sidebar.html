Our Mission

The mission of the Boy Scouts of America is to prepare young people to make ethical and moral choices over their lifetimes by instilling in them the values of the Scout Oath and Law.

Troop 55 is dedicated to the fulfillment of this mission and vision and places a strong emphasis on providing the tools and training for young men to learn to make ethical decisions while having a fun and adventuresome time.

Our Chartering Organization

BSA Troop 55 is supported by our chartering organization, St. Francis Episcopal Church in Great Falls, VA.
