Sign up for August 2025 Outing – Backpacking & Kayaking on the Potomac River

When:  Saturday, August 23rd to Sunday, August 24th

When/Where: Meet at 8AM on Saturday August 23rd at St. Francis Episcopal Church and return to Great Falls Library on Sunday August 24th at 12:00pm

We will meet at St. Francis and drive less than an hour to Sweet Run state park near Harper’s Ferry. We will do light backpacking for about a mile and set up camp. We will then drive 20 minutes to Brunswick family campground for a guided kayak trip for several miles on the Potomac River. This is calm water and the same trip we did last year. We will finish kayaking early in the evening and return to Sweet Run state park to camp overnight.

Scouts and adults participating in the kayaking trip must bring a signed waver form the morning of the outing. The form can be found on the Scoutbook website linked below, as well as further information. Make sure to also RSVP with your status for the outing, also through the Scoutbook website linked below.

Information and registration are found here on Scoutbook calendar.
