<p>On Jan. 7th 2014, six boy scouts from Troop 55 were presented with &#8220;President&#8217;s Volunteer Service Award&#8221; for their extraordinary community service during year 2013 and 2014. Two scouts earned Bronze Medals, one scout achieved the Silver Medal, and three scouts were honored with Gold Medals. &nbsp;Each of them also received a personalized award certificate, and a congratulation letter from President Obama.</p>
<p>Congratulations on these honors for &nbsp;Troop 55!</p>
