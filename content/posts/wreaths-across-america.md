<div>We will be supporting Wreaths Across America again in 2015.&nbsp; Wreaths Across America is a 501 (c)3 organization dedicated to honoring and remembering our veterans.&nbsp;</div>
<div>There are a number of ways&nbsp;to get involved. However, we have elected to support this effort through the purchase of wreaths that will be placed on tombstones at Arlington National Cemetery.&nbsp;</div>
<p><span id="more-3683"></span></p>
<div>For the sponsorship of $15 each, donors will ensure a wreath will mark the grave&nbsp;of one of our many fallen heroes.&nbsp; <b>Checks should be made payable to “Wreaths Across America”</b>. &nbsp;In addition to honoring and remembering our veterans, for each wreath sponsored,&nbsp;$5 will go to the troop to defray the cost of outings as well as to support Eagle Project funds.</div>
<div>&nbsp;</div>
<div><strong>For on line participation</strong> in this worthy cause, g<span style="font-family: Calibri; font-size: medium;">o to </span><a href="http://give.wreathsacrossamerica.org/site/TR?team_id=10358&amp;fr_id=4048&amp;pg=team"><span style="color: #0000ff; font-family: Calibri; font-size: medium;">http://give.<wbr>wreathsacrossamerica.org/goto/<wbr>VA0109P</span></a>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<strong>click on the red button</strong> located on the right side of the screen and titled, <strong>&#8220;Donate to VA0109P &#8211; BSA of America Troop 55!&#8221;. &nbsp;&nbsp;</strong>You will be guided through instructions on how to donate.&nbsp; Please note that in order for individual scouts to receive credit for the donation, you must <strong>include the scout name in the &#8220;Personal Note&#8221; section</strong> of the donation form.&nbsp; Cutoff date is <span class="aBn" tabindex="0" data-term="goog_2112537699"><span class="aQJ">28 November 2015</span></span>.</div>
