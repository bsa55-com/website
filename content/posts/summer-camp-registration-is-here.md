<p>Summer Camp already!?!  Boy howdy, you betcha!</p>
<p>It&#8217;s time to get signed up for Summer Camp, get your physical and put down a deposit/payment.</p>
<p style="text-align: left;" align="center"><b>Summer Camp at Goshen Scout Reservation<br />
</b><b>June 23 – June 29, 2013</b></p>
<p><a href="http://www.boyscouts-ncac.org/goshen-scout-reservation/camps/camp-olmsted/15977">Camp Olmsted at Goshen Scout Reservation</a> is a Boy Scout resident camp located around 450-acre Lake Merriweather in Goshen, Virginia.  Thomas Jefferson once called this area the &#8220;Garden of Eden of the United States&#8221;.  Olmsted offers the open merit badge program which means no pre-sign ups required for merit badges. Scouts simply show up to their desired class with the merit badge card in hand. <b></b></p>
<p><b></b><b>FEES:</b></p>
<p><em id="__mceDel">Scouts $340 (includes round trip bus transportation)  if paid in full by April 19<br />
Adults $280 by April 19<br />
Scouts $380 if paid in full by May 24<br />
Adults $320 if paid in full by May 24<br />
Deposit of $50 due by April 1<b> (non </b>refundable )</em></p>
<p><b>Hat incentive</b> – If your $50 deposit is paid by March 13 you will receive a Camp Goshen hat.  Payment may be made by PayPal on the troop website.</p>
<p>MEDICAL FORMS &#8211; Due June 1.  All scouts and leaders must submit TWO (2) copies of the 2013 Medical Form found on the www.BSA55.com website and signed by a doctor within 12 months of the last day of camp.  The forms can be deposited in the Summer Camp box at all scout meetings. <strong> </strong></p>
