<p>1. I rode the Lochness Monster.</p>
<p>2. I went inside of the Curse of Darkastle</p>
<p>3. I rode the Griffon</p>
<p>4. I ate at the Trapped Smokehouse</p>
<p>5. I rode LE Catapult</p>
<p>6. I went on the Mach Tower</p>
<p>7. I went on Escape from Pompee</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>I went to Busch Gardens. I was with Mrs. Bell and Katie. Iate the Trapped Smokehouse. The food I ate there was a piece of&nbsp;chocolate cake and grapes and a cheeseburger. The funniest part&nbsp;of the trip was when the new scouts and I went on LE Catapult.&nbsp;This ride spin very fast and we had a fun time. Then I took&nbsp;the train to Caribou Train Station. We then rode the Lochness&nbsp;Monster which is my favorite ride. Then we presided to the&nbsp;Curse of Darkastle ride and had a spooktacular time. Then I rode&nbsp;the mother of all rides, The Griffon. We then went on the Mach&nbsp;Tower and then to Escape from Pompee. I rode in the middle.</p>
<p>What I did at the camp site was cook, sleep, and eat, and work on rank advancements for Tender. Will the new scout were&nbsp;doing it to. It was a fun time and all the scouts had a blast.</p>
<p>Nathan H.</p>

<p>Below is Historian Report from Matt O.</p>
<p>The Busch Gardens trip is always a blast.&nbsp; This time we camped at a place called Cheatham Annex which is a run by the Navy.&nbsp; So there were guards with guns at the entrance gate and the adult leaders had to show ID to get in.&nbsp; There must be something important going on at the base. But the important thing is that it is very close to Busch Gardens.&nbsp;&nbsp; We had breakfast and lunch at the campsite.&nbsp; Then the older scouts help instruct the New Scouts and younger scouts on skills.&nbsp; But we were able to leave pretty soon after lunch to Busch Gardens and the Troop stayed until the park closed. We went on a lot of the rides.&nbsp; We liked Griffin because there is a straight drop down.&nbsp; Some of the lines were pretty long so we did not have time for every ride but we got on a bunch and had a great time walking around the park with our buddies. Amazingly, all the scouts made it back to the meeting place at the exit of the park by the deadline.&nbsp; The next morning after breakfast and a service down by the river, we got back into the cars and most of us slept on the way back.</p>
