<h2><span style="color: #ff9900;">THERE HAS NEVER BEEN A JAMBO LIKE THIS. EVER.</span></h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>High adventure like you&#8217;ve never seen.</strong> Whitewater, climbing, zip lines, mountain biking, and much, much more. (Not to mention all of the things you&#8217;ve grown to love about the jamboree: patch trading, SCUBA, arena shows, Technology Quest &#8230; who could ask for more?)</li>
