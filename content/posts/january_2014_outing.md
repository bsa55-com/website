<h1>January Outing – Blue Ridge Center Camping</h1>
<div>
<p><b><a href="http://www.blueridgecenter.org/camping"><img decoding="async" class="alignleft" alt="" src="http://www.blueridgecenter.org/Resources/Pictures/BlueRidge_logo.JPG" width="480" height="163"></a>When:&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</b>January 17-18, 2015<br />
<b>Where: &nbsp;&nbsp;</b>Blue Ridge Center,&nbsp;Purcellville, VA&nbsp;<br />
<b>Time: &nbsp;&nbsp;</b><b>Arrive</b> at the St. Francis parking lot<b> </b>at<b> 7:00 AM on Saturday morning January 17th &#8211;&nbsp;</b><b>Return </b>to St. Francis <b>on Sunday, January 18th</b></p>
<p>The Blue Ridge Center is located on 900 acres of woodlands in an area known as “Between the Hills” (nestled between the Blue Ridge and Short Hill Mountain).&nbsp; Scouts will camp at Demory Field and share views that extend over a meadow and down the Between the Hills valley toward the Potomac River.&nbsp; Demory Field offers potable water, a fire pit, grills, picnic tables, and a large pavilion. Scouts will be geo-caching before lunch.&nbsp; After lunch scouts will hike, work on their scout skills and participate in cold weather Wilderness Survival training and exercises.</p>
