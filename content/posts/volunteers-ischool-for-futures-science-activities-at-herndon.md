<p>Volunteer for&nbsp;iSchool for Future&#8217;s Science Activities&nbsp;at Herndon</p>
<p>Friday, April&nbsp;17, 2015</p>
<p>MOM&#8217;s Organic Market in Herndon (424 Elden St, Herndon, VA 20171)</p>
<p>iSchool for the Future will be offering a few tables with hands-on science activities outside MOM&#8217;s Organic Market in Herndon &nbsp;this upcoming Friday, April 17th, from 11am to 2:30pm. They could use some volunteers to help them set up, take down, and run the activities.&nbsp;<span id="more-3062"></span></p>
<p>As it&#8217;s a day off school in Fairfax, this will be a good opportunity for responsible senior scouts, junior scouts and new scouts&nbsp;to earn up to 4.5 hours of community service. Even if you can&#8217;t make it, please share this call for volunteers with others.</p>
<p>Class A or Class B uniform for the volunteer service event.&nbsp;Volunteers are expected to arrive by 10:30am or 12:30&nbsp;pm, we have two slots options to choose from, you can volunteer for one slot or both slots.</p>
<p>The service hours earned from this vent is qualified for Troop 55 &nbsp;“<a href="/presidents-volunteer-service-award/">President’s Volunteer Service Award</a>” program.</p>
