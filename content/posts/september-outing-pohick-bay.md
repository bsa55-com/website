Time:   <strong>Arrive</strong> at the St. Francis parking lot<strong> </strong><strong>at 7:45 AM on Saturday morning Saturday, Sept. 22nd.</strong></p>

<p>This month the scouts will stay locally with a trip to Camp Wilson at the Pohick Bay Regional Park. The scouts will learn or refresh important scout skills used on many of our more distant outings. We will welcome many of our newest scouts on their first outing with Troop 55. We will bring our trailer with all troop and patrol equipment, and as always, meals are planned and prepared by patrols.  Patrols are responsible for Saturday lunch and dinner, and Sunday breakfast.</p>
