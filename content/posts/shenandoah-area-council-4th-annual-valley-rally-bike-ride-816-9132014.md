This event will be held RAIN OR SHINE and offers a variety of routes including 25, 50, 65, and 100 mile routes. This is a self-paced ride for everyone from the recreational to serious cyclist.</p>
<p>For More information, check the web site at:</p>
<p><a href="http://www.valleyrallybikeride.com/openrosters/view_homepage.aspx?orgkey=2689">http://www.valleyrallybikeride.com/openrosters/view_homepage.aspx?orgkey=2689</a></p>
<p>The Valley Rally Bike Ride is offering another opportunity for Boy Scouts to work towards the Cycling Merit Badge. &nbsp;It covers some of the rides that Boy Scouts need for the Cycling Merit Badge, the Scouts will need additional rides aside from these (3) provided rides to complete the Merit Badge requirements. &nbsp;It will cover the items for the Cycling Merit Badge items 2 &#8211; 5.</p>
<p>The Cycling Merit Badge Ride will be meeting at Clearbrook Park pool entrance in Clear Brook, VA(address: 112 Clearbrook Drive, Clear Brook, VA 22624&nbsp;)&nbsp; at 8:00am each Saturday.&nbsp; The dates are:</p>
<ul>
<li>July 26th</li>
<li><strong>August 16th</strong></li>
<li><strong>September 13th</strong> (4th Annual Valley Rally Bike Ride)</li>
</ul>
<p>The cost is $30/person and it includes support for all 3 rides and a VRBR tshirt.
