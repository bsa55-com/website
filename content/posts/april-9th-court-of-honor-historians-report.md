<p>April 9 Court of Honor:</p>
<p>Historians report</p>
<p><span id="more-2027"></span></p>
<p>Troop 55 held its latest Court of Honor (COH) on April&nbsp;9. Our Troop holds a COH once a quarter. This special&nbsp;event is an opportunity to recognize the accomplishments&nbsp;and achievements of the scouts. Family members are&nbsp;encouraged to attend, especially for scouts who are&nbsp;receiving a rank advancement. Moms (or another adult&nbsp;family member) come up on stage with their scout and&nbsp;receive a rank pin.&nbsp;A total of 18 merit badges and 14 rank advancements&nbsp;were awarded. Way to go scouts!&nbsp;Also at this COH our newest scouts and their families&nbsp;were introduced, and the new scouts received their&nbsp;Handbooks.&nbsp;The last COH for this year is scheduled for Wednesday</p>
<p>June 11, 7:30 PM&#8211;please plan to attend.</p>
