<p>&#8221;&nbsp;I am pleased to announce that at last night’s district annual meeting, the&nbsp;district members-at-large elected Brigadier General Michael C. Dudzik as&nbsp;our new district chairman. General Dudzik is the CEO/President of ERIM,&nbsp;an independent 501 .C.3 with development programs in commercial,&nbsp;medical, automotive and aerospace sectors. He is a former Vice President&nbsp;of Science &amp; Technology for Lockheed Martin’s Washington Operations,&nbsp;where he led an engineering organization responsible for the development&nbsp;of evolutionary and revolutionary technologies for aerospace&nbsp;applications. He currently serves as a committee member in Troop 55&nbsp;Great Falls where his son Andrew is a Scout&#8230; &#8221;</p>
