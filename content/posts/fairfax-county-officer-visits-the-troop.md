<p>During the April 11th meeting Fairfax County Police Officer Twamley visited with the troop to supplement the month&#8217;s theme of <em>Public Service</em>.  Officer Twamley discussed the training and education it takes to become a police officer and shared some interesting anecdotes with the boys.  He then opened it up for questions.</p>
<p>Several of the new drivers in the troop had traffic-related and new driver questions.  Many of the boys were fascinated with pepper spray and methods for apprehending a &#8220;bad guy&#8221;.  There were multiple questions as to whether Officer Twamley had ever &#8220;tased&#8221; anyone, to which he replied <em>No</em>.  There was a momentary buzz of excitement when it was suggested Scoutmaster Pan might volunteer to receive a mild &#8220;tase&#8221;, but unfortunately Officer Twamley did not have his taser with him.</p>
<p>Troop 55 looks forward to another visit from Officer Twamley!  Many thanks to scout Paul Y. for coordinating the visit.</p>
