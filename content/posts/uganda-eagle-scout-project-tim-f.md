<p style="text-align: left;" align="center"><b>Uganda Eagle Scout Project</b></p>
<p>Every year 600,000 people die from malaria in Sub-Saharan Africa. In order to prevent the spread malaria I am asking for your help. My Eagle Scout project is to support a Scout troop based in Kampala, Uganda by collecting new or gently used camping equipment. My goal is to collect <b>30 tents, 30 sleeping bags, 10 hiking backpacks, 40 mess kits and most importantly 50 Mosquito Nets. </b></p>
<p>There are several ways you can contribute:</p>
<ul>
<li>Examining your storage space for the above items.</li>
<li>Local purchases or from my gift registry at REI <a href="http://www.rei.com/GiftRegistryDetails/GR6118525">http://www.rei.com/GiftRegistryDetails/GR6118525</a></li>
<li>Monetary donations for additional purchases and to cover shipping costs</li>
</ul>
<p>My collection will end by <em>October 31, 2013</em>. If you have any questions please email me at <a href="javascript:DeCryptX('0t0i3p0f3h0n2i1834270@3j0m2c2k1m1/3f3r3p')">timfeng715 [at] gmail [dot] com</a>. Please help me help these Scouts living in Uganda who are fighting for their lives.</p>
