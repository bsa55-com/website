<h1>Fourth of July Honor Guard at the National Parade</h1>
<p>It&#8217;s not too late! &nbsp;If you are interested at all in joining Troop 55 at the National Parade, fill out the form below, scan it and email it to: &nbsp;<a href="javascript:DeCryptX('0j1v3o2k0a0.0t1i3r3u1t1f2p3C0g0m2c3l2n203f0o1n')">Mrs. Thorsen</a></p>
<p>Troop 55 is scheduled to serve as the honor guard for the Fourth of July Parade in Washington, DC. We need quite a few volunteers to confirm an impressive display. We will leave from St. Francis about 8am on Thursday morning, July 4th, and will return in the mid afternoon. Please join us for this special event. Class A+ required.</p>
<p><em><strong>Please Note:</strong> &nbsp;This year a release form is required for all participants. &nbsp;Even if you are only interested in coming but can&#8217;t commit yet, please submit a release form. &nbsp;Scouts and adults who do not submit release forms ahead of time will &nbsp;not be permitted to participate.</em></p>
