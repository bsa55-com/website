<p>During the May Outing, the Scorpions went to Maryland and West Virginia to do canoeing and whitewater rafting. During whitewater rafting, we went through rapids ranging from Class 1-4. The rafts all fought in splash wars, getting us drenched in water in the end.<span id="more-5666"></span></p>
<p>For dinner, the Scorpions are pineapples with chicken and jalapeños. We were lucky that our stove was working. Some other patrols had it leaking.</p>
