<p>Today we went on a very fun trip or rock climb. It was 300 feet high, and everyone couldn’t wait to climb it. We were all excited to climb. All of us had to be in three lines, so when other people fall, they don’t fall on us. One thing is that none of us actually fell, and we were the only team that had done the trip fully without anybody leaving during the middle.</p>
<p>It was so cold on the top of the mountain that we were all shivering, and Barry even had a stomach ache that he yelled about all the way to the end. <span id="more-5348"></span>After the rock climb, there was the zipline for young kids. It was also very cold on the zip line, but fun. Sometimes we could zipline three times in a row but sometimes we only had one time. After each run, we had to wait for a long time, that was the part I hated the most. But for most of the time I thought it was a fun trip and we enjoyed it a lot.</p>
<p>We should have more of these trips, we really enjoyed it!<br />
