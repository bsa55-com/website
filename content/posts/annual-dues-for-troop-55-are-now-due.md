<p style="text-align: left;"><strong>ANNUAL DUES FOR TROOP 55 ARE NOW DUE!</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><strong>DUES FOR 2015-16 ARE $175.00 FOR THE YEAR.</strong></p>
<p>Dues cover the following benefits:</p>
<ul>
<li>Official annual registration fees with national Boy Scouts of America</li>
<li>All awards, rank advancements, merit badges and patches</li>
<li>Court of Honor Expenses (including fully catered fall CoH)</li>
<li>Scout leadership training</li>
<li>Convenience of PayPal service to allow credit card payments&nbsp;without transaction fees</li>
<li>Troop website costs for instant access to calendar and links to&nbsp;registration/sign-up for all scout events</li>
<li>Maintenance/upgrade of extensive troop equipment, including all patrol&nbsp;cooking gear, camp stoves, fuel, camp tools and troop trailer</li>
<li>Annual swim test fees</li>
</ul>
<p><span id="more-3646"></span></p>
<p>In addition, fees include:</p>
<ul>
<li>All adult leadership/safety training</li>
<li>Summer camp fees for adults</li>
</ul>
<p>In an effort to boost our participation in Friends of Scouting (FoS), families can&nbsp;donate now to FoS when they pay their dues, and the Troop will transfer the funds&nbsp;to FoS. However, to receive the tax deduction for a charitable contribution, you&nbsp;must make the donation directly to Friends of Scouting. Information for donating&nbsp;directly may be found at <a href="http://www.ncacbsa.org/?page=FOS%20">http://www.ncacbsa.org/?page=FOS </a>&nbsp; .</p>
<p>All families donating to Friends of Scouting will be listed in the Court of Honor&nbsp;programs throughout the year. Funding Friends of Scouting is a necessary&nbsp;component to the success of the National Capital Area Council (NCAC) of BSA, and&nbsp;ultimately your Scout.</p>
<p>Bring a check to the Wednesday meeting payable to “BSA Troop 55”&nbsp;or go to the PayPal link in upper right corner of the website!</p>
