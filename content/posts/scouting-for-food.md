<p>Scouting for food is scheduled for the Saturdays of November 5th and 12th. On the 5th we will need volunteers to help drop bags around our designated neighborhood. On the 12 th we will need scouts to go back and pick up the filled bags and other volunteers to be in front of Safeway for our drive. We are collecting for <a title="Share of McLean" href="http://www.shareofmclean.org/Home/tabid/108/Default.aspx">SHARE</a> and they will have a truck at Safeway on the 12th from 9-5 ( or whenever we wrap up.)</p>
