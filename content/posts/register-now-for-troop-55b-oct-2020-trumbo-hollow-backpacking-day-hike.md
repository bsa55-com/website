<p><strong>When</strong>:&nbsp; October 17-18, 2019<br />
<strong>Where</strong>: Trumbo Hollow,&nbsp;VA<br />
<strong>Time</strong>:&nbsp;Arrive at the St. Francis parking lot at 6:30 AM on Saturday morning, October 17th<br />
Return to St. Francis by late afternoon&nbsp;on Sunday, October 18th(Backpack)</p>
<p>A beautiful out and back hike on the Appalachian Trail passing through fields, streams, lush forest, and the Jim and Molly Denton Shelter. Trumbo Hollow is an expansive meadow with a bench at the high point. With fields and fences that make you feel like you&#8217;re in a J.R.R. Tolkien novel the hike down towards VA638 is one more Kodak moment. From the valley enjoy another forest walk before arriving at the Jim and Molly Denton Shelter which boasts a picnic pavilion and gravity fed shower.&nbsp;<a title="Register Now For Oct. 2020 Trumbo Hollow Trip" href="/oct-2020-troop-55b-outing-trumbo-hollow-backpacking-dayhike/">Click here to see all the details!</a></p>
