<p>Oct 22-23, 2011:  Dolly Sods Backpacking:On our October outing in Dolly Sods the Dragons patrol had a phenomenal time.  We parked at Bear Rocks and hit the trail around 12:30.  On this rather blustery day we hiked to the top of the Red Creek trail where we camped.  Our campsite was one of the best yet, the stone seats were a patrol favorite.  We woke up early the next morning, which was a bit nippy.  After a warm breakfast of oatmeal, we hiked out to the Blackbird Knob trailhead and shuttled the cars.  I think that we&#8217;d all agree this was a great trip.</p>
<p>Contributed by Troop Historian: Kyle</p>
