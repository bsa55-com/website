<p><strong><a href="/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/gettysburgimage.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-2850 alignleft" alt="gettysburgimage" src="/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/gettysburgimage-300x77.jpg" width="300" height="77" srcset="/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/gettysburgimage-300x77.jpg 300w, /wp-content/uploads/2015/02/gettysburgimage.jpg 361w" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" /></a>When:</strong>&nbsp;April 11-12, 2015<br />
<strong>Where:&nbsp;</strong>&nbsp;Artillery Ridge Campground, Gettysburg, PA<br />
<strong>Time:</strong>&nbsp;Arrive at the St. Francis parking lot at 6:30 am on Saturday, April 11, 2015 Return to St. Francis mid-afternoon on Sunday, April 12, 2015, approx time 1pm</p>
<p>Troop 55 will travel to Gettysburg, PA and learn about the Battle of Gettysburg, a turning point in the Civil War, the setting for President Abraham Lincoln&#8217;s &#8220;Gettysburg Address&#8221;. The group will camp at Artillery Ridge Campground, conduct an orienteering instructional session, see a short movie at the visitor’s center, then the group will split &#8211; some will go on a biking tour through the battlefield (14 miles), and the rest will do a 4 mile hike. All bikers must have helmets, and have bikes that are capable of this ride.</p>
<p><span id="more-2843"></span><br />
We will bring our trailer with all troop and patrol equipment, and as always, meals are planned and prepared by patrols. Scouts need to have a day pack, with Lunch and water in it for the days activities. Patrols are responsible for Saturday dinner, and Sunday breakfast. Scouts should either eat breakfast at home or bring a meal for the car on Saturday morning. Scouts should remember the potential need for layered clothing when they pack, and they should bring a warm sleeping bag. Remember, rain gear and sunscreen are essential!</p>
