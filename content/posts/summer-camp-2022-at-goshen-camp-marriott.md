<p><span style="background-color: #ffff00;"><b>June 26-July 2, 2022</b></span></p>
<p>Camp Marriott offers camping for 300 participants and includes an extensive aquatics program including sailing, canoeing, kayaking, rowing, and the ever popular water sports merit badge. Also of note is our ecology area, where there is an ever-changing zoo to showcase the flora and fauna of Goshen. Camp Marriott is located near great hiking trails, and offers the option of Patrol Cooking or Heater Stack dining options.</p>
