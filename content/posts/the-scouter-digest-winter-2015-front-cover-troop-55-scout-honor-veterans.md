<p>Boy Scout from BSA Troop 55 was on the front cover of the Winter 2015 edition of &#8220;The Scouter Digest (National Capital Area Council)&#8221;. &nbsp;Scouts participated the NCAC &#8216;s &#8220;Veterans Day &nbsp;Wreath Laying&#8221; service at Washington DC , &nbsp;met with veterans and passed out roses to them as a way of thanking them for their service to our country. &nbsp;</p>
