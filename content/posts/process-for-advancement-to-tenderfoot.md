<p>It was WONDERFUL to see so many new scouts at the instructor session this past Wednesday. &nbsp;We’ve had some queries about how to advance to Tenderfoot. &nbsp;As this rank advancement is a bit more complicated than the “Scout” rank, we wanted to provide the steps to get there. &nbsp;Please encourage your son to reach to the Troop Guides (Mitchell Pan and Tim Fahlgren) initially, then Patrol Advisors for any questions, but never hesitate to ask a question of anyone.</p>
<p>To reach Tenderfoot rank:</p>
<p><span id="more-2309"></span><br />
1.&nbsp; Regularly attend the Wednesday meetings following an outing (instructor session) in your Class A uniform and bring your book-the focus is on completing requirements below First Class.</p>
<p>2. Find Scouts who are First Class and above and have them sign off on completed requirements in your book.&nbsp;</p>
<p>3.&nbsp; Send an email to your Patrol Advisor (listed at the end of this email, and on the website) &#8211; that you would like a Pre-Scoutmaster&#8217;s Conference &#8211; they will work with you to move to Tenderfoot.</p>
<p>4.&nbsp; Print out this form and complete the section for Tenderfoot:<br />
<a href="http://bsa55com.dot5hosting.com/wp-content/uploads/downloads/2011/06/Pre-Scoutmasters-Conference-Form.pdf">http://bsa55com.dot5hosting.com/wp-content/uploads/downloads/2011/06/Pre-Scoutmasters-Conference-Form.pdf</a><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><br />
</span><br />
5.&nbsp; If you have all requirements signed off and your Pre-Scoutmaster conference worksheet with your Patrol Advisor completed, tell Mr. Pan or Mr. Reuss you are ready for a Scoutmaster conference. &nbsp;This will occur on an outing.</p>
<p>6. You&#8217;ll then contact the Troop Board of Review committee chair (Mr. Andersen) to schedule your board of review. Board of Reviews are completed once a month during the PLC/Instructor session on the Wednesday following an outing. <a href="file://diskstation/NetworkHD2TB_SHARE/JeffDocuments/BSA/WEBsite/assets/troop55bor [at] gmail [dot] com">troop55bor [at] gmail [dot] com</a></p>
