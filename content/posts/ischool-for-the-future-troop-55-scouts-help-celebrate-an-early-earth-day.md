<p>Troop 55 scout Matthew M. wrote a short report for this community service event:</p>
<p><em>Volunteering at the iSchool for the future™ event was a good opportunity and experience. The event was to help kids learn about science. &nbsp;They had displays on composting, pH, potato batteries, paper helicopters, and water runoff. &nbsp;</em></p>
<p><em>I helped set up and worked at the station on pH, &nbsp;Grant S. came later and helped too, and&nbsp;our main&nbsp;action was on setup and takedown. &nbsp; Kids liked the colorful liquids at the pH station, and the potato station got some interesting questions from adults. &nbsp;</em></p>
<p><em>I felt like it was a great choice to go there because you got a sense of satisfaction by helping others.</em></p>
