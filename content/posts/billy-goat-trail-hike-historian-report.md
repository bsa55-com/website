<p>Troop 55 had a wonderful outing last weekend just across the river in&nbsp;Potomac, Maryland.&nbsp; Our activity for this outing was the Billy Goat trail,&nbsp;featuring a rocky pathway along the Potomac River and fantastic views of&nbsp;the falls.&nbsp; Even though it was so close to home this was a backpacking&nbsp;outing and the scouts used hiking stoves and packed all their gear into&nbsp;backpacks.&nbsp;<span id="more-4072"></span></p>
<p>We also focused on advancement for the scouts mostly among the&nbsp;younger kids.&nbsp; Progress towards tenderfoot, first class, and second class is&nbsp;increasing steadily and many of our newer scouts are advancing quickly. &nbsp;This was also the first outing with our new scouts who all had a great time&nbsp;thanks to the help of Brandon Boose and Nick Hodge, even though we did&nbsp;have a few scares.&nbsp; Overall it was a great outing and many of the older&nbsp;scouts even cannot wait to come back next year.</p>
