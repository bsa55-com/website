<p>When:  June 14 – 16, 2013<br />
Where: <a href="http://www.knoebels.com/">Knoebels Amusement Park</a><br />
Time:   <b>Arrive</b> at the St. Francis parking lot<b> </b><b>at 5:00 PM on Friday, June 14th<br />
</b><b>Return </b>to St. Francis mid-afternoon<b> on Sunday, June 16th</b></p>
<p>Troop 55 will camp out on Friday and Saturday night at Twin Grove Campground, 105-acres nestled in the rolling Blue Mountains of Pennsylvania. On Saturday morning scouts will have chances to assist each other in advancement activities. Patrol Leaders and youth leaders are urged to attend. After lunch the troop will drive to <a href="http://www.knoebels.com/">Knoebels Amusement park</a> to enjoy the rides and shows and stay until closing. (10PM)</p>
