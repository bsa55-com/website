<p>Wreaths Across America is a 501 (c)3 organization dedicated to honoring and remembering our veterans. &nbsp;Each December on National Wreaths Across America Day, the mission&nbsp;of Wreaths Across America to <em>Remember, Honor and Teach</em> is carried out by coordinating wreath-laying ceremonies at Arlington National Cemetery, as well as over 1,100 additional locations in all 50 U.S. states, at sea, and abroad.</p>
<p>&nbsp;Troop 55 will support this effort through the purchase of wreaths that will be placed on tombstones at Arlington National Cemetery.&nbsp; For the sponsorship of $15 each, donors will ensure a wreath will mark the grave of one of our many fallen heroes.&nbsp; In addition to honoring and remembering our veterans, for each wreath sponsored, $5 will go to Troop 55 to defray the cost of outings as well as to support Eagle Project funds.</p>
