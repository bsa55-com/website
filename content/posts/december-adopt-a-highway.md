<p>On Saturday December 6, 2014 the Dragons and Eagles patrols participated in our Troop&#8217;s monthly Adopt a Highway. Starting out at 9:30 am behind the Dominos, they planned out how they would complete the segment of Walker Road all the way to approximately River Bend Country Club. &nbsp;The Dragons and the Eagles patrols split up and started at both ends; meeting in the middle to finish the job. Luckily they were able to finish around 11:30 am get back to the cars just as the rain started. <a href="https://troop55greatfallsva.shutterfly.com/pictures/13406">HERE</a> is a link to pictures from the event. In the end, LOTS of garbage was collected.</p>
