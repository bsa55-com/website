<p>On March 20th 2014, Boys Scouts from Troop 55 helped Forestville Elementary School run its first international Math Kangaroo competition.&nbsp;</p>
<p>&#8221;&nbsp;Boys Scouts from Troop 55 assisted with check-in, seating, and&nbsp;maintaining the test environment, as well as official photographers for&nbsp;the event. One Troop 55 Boy Scout was tasked with being the school&nbsp;mascot &#8211; the &#8220;Cardinal&#8221; – keeping the environment fun and enjoyable&nbsp;for the participating students. &#8221;</p>
