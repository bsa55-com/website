<p><a href="/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/YPTlogo.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" size-full wp-image-5206 alignleft" src="/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/YPTlogo.jpg" alt="YPTlogo" width="191" height="166"></a>In May, BSA released updates to the Youth Protection Training program.&nbsp; It is MANDATORY that all adult leaders and parents who go on outings and events update their YPT by <strong>September 30, 2018</strong>.&nbsp; &nbsp;To access the training, use the <a href="https://my.scouting.org/">my.scouting.org</a> site.&nbsp; Review this&nbsp;<a href="https://www.scouting.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/How-to-Guide-for-Taking-Youth-Protection-Training-as-of-5-16-2018.pdf">How-To guide</a>&nbsp;if you have questions about how to access the online training, and see or contact Mr. Anderson if you need your BSA ID number.</p>
<p>When complete, please send your training certificate to Marie Gordon.</p>
<p><strong>NOTE:</strong>&nbsp; <strong>For any adult who plans to attend Troop 55&#8217;s first outing of the year (Dolly Sods in September), you should complete your updated training no later than Friday, September 14, 2018.</strong></p>
