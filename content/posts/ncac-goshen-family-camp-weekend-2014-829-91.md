<p><a href="http://www.ncacbsa.org/events/event_details.asp?id=476243&amp;group="><img decoding="async" class="alignleft" style="margin-left: 5px; margin-right: 5px;" alt="Goshen Family Camp 2014" src="http://www.ncacbsa.org/resource/events/20140728_115150_29608.jpg" width="160" height="188"></a>All Scouts and families are invited to join BSA National Capital Area Council&#8217;s &nbsp;Family Camping Weekend at Goshen Scout Reservation.</p>
<p>The weekend begins on&nbsp;<strong>Friday, August 29th</strong>&nbsp;and continues through&nbsp;<strong>Monday, September 1st, 2014</strong>.</p>
<p>The cost is&nbsp;<strong>$15.00 per person</strong>. Your family will be able participate in activities such as: BB Gun, Rifle&nbsp;and Shotgun Shooting, Swimming, Archery, Waterskiing and Wakeboarding, hiking, campfires,other staff-led activities. Each family in turn provides their own tents, food, and personal items.</p>
