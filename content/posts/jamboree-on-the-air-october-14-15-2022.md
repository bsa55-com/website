<div class="entry-content">
<p><strong>Jamboree on the Air</strong>, or JOTA, is the largest Scouting event in the world and uses amateur radio to link scouts and ham radio operators throughout the world. Scouts who arrive on Friday evening will setup for JOTA then enjoy flashlight code competition and camping.  Events on Saturday include a flag code competition, a foxhunt radio signal competition, and Scouts on the Air.</p>
