<p>Troop 55 Lead Historian Corey H. wrote an article for the Connection newspaper profiling Troop 55&#8217;s most recent scouts to achieve the rank of Eagle Scout.  Click on the link below to read the full article:</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.connectionnewspapers.com/news/2012/jun/27/seven-troop-55-boy-scouts-earn-eagle-scout/">http://www.connectionnewspapers.com/news/2012/jun/27/seven-troop-55-boy-scouts-earn-eagle-scout/</a></strong></p>
<p>&#8220;Completing over 150 merit badges and more than 2,100 requirements collectively, seven Troop 55 Boy Scouts achieved the rank of Eagle Scout in Great Falls. Clayton Barber, Kyle Dear, Brad Hodge, Garrett Pan, Neil Patil, Grant Smith and John Studabaker joined the less than 2 percent of Boy Scouts who reach the rank of Eagle Scout. These Scouts each completed a service project, serving as project manager for more than 100 man-hours of volunteer time to improve their community.&#8221;</p>
