<p align="center"><b><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Twin River Camping and Canoe Trip Sept 20-21, 2014</span></b></p>
<p align="center">&nbsp;</p>
<p>When: Sept 20-21, 2014<br />
Where: &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Twin River Outfitters: Breeden Bottom Campground and James River Canoeing<br />
Time: &nbsp; Arrive at the St. Francis parking lot at <b>6:30 am </b>on Saturday, Sept 20<sup>th</sup> and return to St. Francis mid-afternoon on &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Sunday, Sept 21st</p>
<p><span id="more-2253"></span></p>
<p>Whether this is your first canoe trip or you&#8217;re an experienced boater, this 10.5-mile 2-man canoe trip is ideal for a large group wanting to have fun.&nbsp; It includes plenty of rapids; 5 which are rated class II, great mountain views, and a remote wilderness feel.&nbsp; On Saturday you will check in with Twin River Outfitters, where you will receive your safety orientation, river maps, and life jacket. From there you&#8217;ll be shuttled to the staging area where you&#8217;ll begin your 2-day river trip. Sunday morning you will complete the rest of the 10.5 mile canoe trip after breakfast, clean up and head home.</p>
