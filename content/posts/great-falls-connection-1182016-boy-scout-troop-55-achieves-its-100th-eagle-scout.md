Boy Scout Troop 55, sponsored by St. Francis Episcopal Church in Great Falls, promoted five scouts to the Eagle Rank. The new Eagle Scouts are Andrew Dudzik, Corey Hodge, Joseph Kee and Paul Youssef, all attending Langley High School; and Nick Dunie who attends Fort Union Military Academy. Each of these Eagle Scouts demonstrated service to the community, leadership and mentored other scouts. In addition, they each planned and executed a complex project as described below.</p>
