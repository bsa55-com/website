<p>Slots:&nbsp;5:00-6:00: Set up;</p>
<p>5:30-7:30: Orientation and Running Stations;</p>
<p>7:30-8:30: Take down and clean up.</p>
<p>Refreshments will be provided during orientation. Volunteers are requested for the entire slot, but we can work with partial time commitments. There will be an orientation for volunteers running each station, and requests to rotate stations will be handled then. Please indicate partial time needs in &#8220;My comments&#8221;.</p>
