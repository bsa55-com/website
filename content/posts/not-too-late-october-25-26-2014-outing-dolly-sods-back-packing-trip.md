<p>When: October 25-26, 2014<br />
Where: Dolly Sods Wilderness, Monongahela National Forest, WV<br />
Time: <strong>Arrive</strong> at the St. Francis parking lot <strong>at 6:30 AM on Saturday morning Saturday, October 25th</strong><br />
<strong>Return</strong> to St. Francis by late afternoon on <strong>Sunday, October 26nd</strong></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Dolly Sods and its’ environs are considered the premier backpacking locales east of the Mississippi River. They feature varied terrain and eco-zones more reminiscent of parts of Canada than the mid-Atlantic. The scouts will backpack and camp in patrols along Red Creek or along the many scenic meadows above the bogs of the wilderness.</p>
