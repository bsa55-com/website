<p><strong>When:</strong>&nbsp;June 7-8, 2014<br />
<strong>Where:&nbsp;</strong>&nbsp;Antietam Battle Field, Sharpsburg, PA<br />
<strong>Time:</strong>&nbsp;&nbsp;<b>Arrive</b>&nbsp;at the St. Francis parking lot&nbsp;<b>at 6:30 for a 7:00 am departure, Saturday, June 7th<br />
</b><b>Return&nbsp;</b>to St. Francis around noon<b>&nbsp;on Sunday, June 8th</b></p>
<p><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><a title="Antietam battlefield" href="http://www.nps.gov/anti/index.htm">The Antietam Battlefield</a>&nbsp;</span>&nbsp;where the one day bloodiest battle in American History occurred in 1862, &nbsp;is only 1 hour and a half away from us. &nbsp;The boys will be choosing between a 25 mile bike ride along the potomac and through the battlefield, or a 10 mile hike through the battlefield. &nbsp;In both cases scouts will be carrying light backpacks with lunch and will tour the Antietam Museum. &nbsp;We will be staying at the primitive <a title="info on the campground" href="http://www.nps.gov/anti/planyourvisit/campgrounds.htm">Rohrbach Campground </a>on the battlefield. &nbsp;If your scout is doing the biking portion, they must provide their own bike and helmet. &nbsp;Each scout should have a spare tube that fits their bike, adults will have pumps and repair kits.</p>
