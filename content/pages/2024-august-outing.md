<p>No Lunch is served on the river<br />
3 to 3.5 hour trip</p>
<p>The Lower Yough is 7.5 miles of Class III – III + whitewater rapids. It is considered a “drop &amp; pool” river: the rapids are separated by calm deep pools, allowing you to catch your breath and enjoy the scenery. It’s a thrilling whitewater experience &amp; full of adventure! <strong>Participants should be at least 10 years old with some paddling experience.</strong></p>
<p>Enjoy an exciting day on a 7.5 mile Class III rafting trip where the scenery is beautiful and rapids are challenging. The Lower Yough is a Drop &amp; Pool river, which means every whitewater rapid is separated by a calm deep pool, allowing you to catch your breath, goof with friends, start a water fight and prepare for the next wave of fun! If you are 10 and older or a first time rafter, this is the perfect river trip for you. No meals included.</p>
<p><strong>This is a fully guided trip with 6-7 individuals in each raft including the guide.</strong></p>

<p>We will bring troop first-aid kid and some extra masks, meals are planned and prepared by individual scouts. Scouts please eat breakfast at home, bring a bag lunch in a water-proof bag for Sunday lunch and some extra snacks.  Scouts may want to bring a snack to eat before the return trip and/or money for a snack if the driver stop. Scouts should remember to protect themselves from sun, mosquitos and other bugs by bringing appropriate spray, as well as <strong>extra pairs of dry clothes</strong>. Rain gear is always a good idea — even if it doesn’t rain, it is great wind protection. Please bring 2 water bottles and close toe shoe for canoeing safety. Bring a dry-bag to store away cellphone, lunch, extra dry cloth, sun screen and snacks on the river.</p>
