<p><img decoding="async" class="alignleft" style="margin: 2px; border: 0px currentColor;" src="/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/dolly-sods-mountains-sunny_sm.jpg" alt="" width="300" />When:  October 12-13, 2024<br />
Where: Dolly Sods Wilderness, Monongahela National Forest, WV<br />
Time: <strong>Arrive</strong> at the St. Francis parking lot <b>at 6:30 AM on Saturday morning, October 12th</b><br />
<strong>Return </strong>to Great Falls Library by late afternoon<strong> on Sunday, October 13<b>th</b></strong></p>
<p><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dolly_Sods_Wilderness">Dolly Sods</a> and its’ environs are considered the premier backpacking locales east of the Mississippi River. They feature varied terrain and eco-zones more reminiscent of parts of Canada than the mid-Atlantic. The scouts will backpack and camp in patrols along Red Creek or along the many scenic meadows above the bogs of the wilderness.</p>
<p>Scouts must be prepared for creek crossings and cold nights when we camp. This means a change into warm dry clothes (no cotton) for sleeping, extra layers (no cotton) to add on as we climb in elevation, water shoes, walking stick (if you have one), and extra pairs of dry socks (no cotton) if they get wet on the crossings.  You should have <span style="text-decoration: underline;">a minimum</span> of two, preferably three, 1-liter Nalgene bottles filled with water when we meet at the church. As always, the boys must bring rain gear for both the upper and lower body.</p>
<p>Scouts must arrive at St. Francis at 6:30 a.m. Saturday morning after having breakfast. They must bring <span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>a personal trail lunch</strong></span> for the hike on Saturday. Patrols will prepare Saturday dinner and Sunday breakfast. Scouts should bring money for a quick lunch stop for the return trip on Sunday. Return is estimated by 2 p.m. Sunday.  Scouts will call their parents as they near the library.</p>
<p>There is a <strong>$50 fee</strong> for each scout and parent attending (which includes a $25 transportation fee). The transportation fee is waived for both scout and parent if the parent is driving.</p>
