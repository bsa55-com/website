<p><strong>When</strong>: Feb 24-25, 2024<br />
<strong>Where</strong>:<br />
<strong>Camping: </strong> <strong>Robert C McDonell Campground</strong>, 7701 Tuckerman Ln, Rockville, MD 20854<br />
<strong>Skating:  Cabin John Ice Rink</strong> &#8211; 10610 Westlake Dr, Rockville, MD 20852<br />
<strong>Time</strong>:<br />
<strong>Arrive</strong><span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span>at St. Francis church at 9:30 am on Saturday, February 24<br />
<strong>Return</strong> to Great Falls Library around noon on Sunday, February 25</p>
<p>Scouts will enjoy car camping at Robert C McDonell Campground in Rockville, MD on Saturday night after ice skating at Cabin John Ice Rink  from 12:00PM to 2PM.</p>
<p>Scouts should pack a <strong>bag lunch for Saturday</strong>. We will bring our trailer with all troop and patrol equipment, and as always, <strong>Saturday dinner and Sunday breakfast</strong> are planned and prepared by patrols. Patrols will be responsible for dinner on Saturday, and Sunday breakfast. <strong>Scouts can bring gloves and bicycle or ski helmet for skating protection.</strong></p>
<p>Scouts must be prepared for cold weather and should remember the potential need for <strong>layered, synthetic clothing</strong> when they pack. <strong>Rain gear</strong> and a <strong>warm sleeping bag</strong> are a must, as well as a change into warm dry clothes (no cotton) for sleeping, extra layers (no cotton) to add on, and extra pairs of dry socks. It will be cold in the evening.</p>
<p>There is a <strong>$50 fee</strong> for each scout and parent attending. THE  <strong>$15 TRANSPORTATION FEE</strong> IS WAIVED FOR BOTH SCOUT AND PARENT IF THE PARENT IS DRIVING.<strong>  </strong></p>
