<h3>Check out the list of places Troop 55 scouts have visited through BSA High Adventure!</h3>
<p>Troop 55 has a long history of sending scouts to High Adventure &#8211; leaving our scouts with lasting memories and fostering development in character and leadership.</p>
<h3><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>Recent High Adventure trips</strong></span></h3>
<ul>
<li>Winter Break 2021:  Florida Keys &#8211; Scuba diving adventure</li>
<li>Spring Break 2021:  Florida Keys &#8211; Scuba diving adventure</li>
<li>Spring Break 2021:  Florida Keys &#8211; Out island adventur</li>
<li>Winter Break 2020:  Florida Keys &#8211; Coral reef sailing (55G)</li>
<li>Winter Break 2020:  Florida Keys &#8211; Scuba Live Aboard (55B)</li>
<li>Winter Break 2020:  Florida Keys &#8211; Tall Ship Sailing (55B)</li>
<li>Winter Break 2019:  Michigan Norther Tier &#8211; Winter Cold Weather Camping (55B)</li>
<li>Spring Break 2019:  Florida Keys &#8211; Coral reef sailing</li>
<li>Spring Break 2019:  Florida Keys &#8211; Out island Adventure</li>
<li>Summer 2019:  World Scout Jamboree (The Summit &#8211; Betchtel Scout Reserve, West Virginia)</li>
<li>Summer 2019:  Philmont backpacking</li>
<li>Summer 2018:  Cycling Across America (15 States from Seattle, WA to Great Falls, VA) -cycling &amp; camping</li>
<li>Summer 2018:  Northern Tier (Minnesota &amp; Canada) &#8211; canoeing &amp; fishing</li>
<li>Summer 2017:  National Scout Jamboree (The Summit &#8211; Betchtel Scout Reserve, West Virginia)</li>
<li>Summer 2017:  Florida Keys &#8211; Sea Base scuba adventure</li>
<li>Spring Break 2017:  Great Abaco Island, Bahamas &#8211; sailing &amp; snorkeling</li>
<li>Winter 2016:  InterAmerica Scouts Jamboree (Guayaquil, Ecuador)</li>
<li>Summer 2016:  Northern Tier (Minnesota &amp; Canada) &#8211; canoeing &amp; fishing</li>
<li>Summer 2016:  Philmont Scout Ranch of New Mexico &#8211; backpacking &amp; hiking</li>
<li>Summer 2016:  Swamp Base (Lafeyette, Louisianna) &#8211; canoeing &amp; fishing</li>
<li>Summer 2016:  International Scouts Jamboree (Ulfljotsvatn, Iceland)</li>
</ul>
