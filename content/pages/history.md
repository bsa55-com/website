<p><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>History of Troop 55<br />
</strong></span>Troop 55, first chartered in 1933, is one of the&nbsp;largest Boy Scout troops in the United States. We generally have&nbsp;around 60 registered Scouts organized into small patrols. Our Scouts attend many different&nbsp;public and private schools in and around the Great Falls, VA area and come from a variety of faiths.</p>
<p><strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Outings<br />
</span></strong>Troop 55 goes on a variety of outings throughout the course of the year. &nbsp;Our scouts are active in high adventure and attend a week-long summer camp program.</p>

<p><strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Cooking and Recipe Resource</span><br />
</strong>The Scouts of Troop 55 are cooking&nbsp;aficionados. &nbsp;We put our supply of dutch ovens to use at almost every outing. &nbsp;Each December, in a Troop 55 tradition, we have a troop-wide &#8220;Cooking Competition&#8221; at St. Francis Church. &nbsp;The recipes and meal favorites celebrate the diversity and cultural heritage of our troop.</p>
