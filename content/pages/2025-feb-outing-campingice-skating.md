<p><strong>When</strong>: Feb 15-16, 2025</p>
<p><strong>Where</strong>:  <strong>Camping: </strong> Camp Snyder, 6100<span class="LrzXr"> Antioch Rd, Haymarket, VA 20169, </span><strong>Skating:  Reston Town Center Pavillion</strong>, 1818 Discovery St, Reston, VA 20190 &#8211;</p>
<p><strong>Time</strong>:  <strong>Arrive</strong> at St. Francis church at 10:00 am on Saturday, February 15, <strong>Return</strong> to Great Falls Library around noon on Sunday, February 25</p>
<p>Scouts will enjoy camping at Camp William B. Snyder in Haymarket, Virginia starting Saturday afternoon after ice skating at the Reston Town Center.</p>
<p>Scouts should pack a <strong>bag lunch for Saturday</strong>. We will bring our trailer with all troop and patrol equipment, and as always, <strong>Saturday dinner and Sunday breakfast</strong> are planned and prepared by patrols. <strong>Scouts should dress appropriately and may bring gloves and bicycle or ski helmet for skating protection.</strong></p>
<p>Scouts must be prepared for cold weather and should remember the potential need for <strong>layered, synthetic clothing</strong> when they pack. <strong>Rain gear</strong> and a <strong>warm sleeping bag</strong> are a must, as well as a change into warm dry clothes (no cotton) for sleeping, extra layers (no cotton) to add on, and extra pairs of dry socks. It will be cold in the evening.</p>
<p>There is a <strong>$50 fee</strong> for each scout and parent attending. THE  <strong>$15 TRANSPORTATION FEE</strong> IS WAIVED FOR BOTH SCOUT AND PARENT IF THE PARENT IS DRIVING.<strong>  </strong></p>
