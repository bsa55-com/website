<p><strong><br />
</strong>Includes Saturday camping site,  dinner on Saturday, breakfast on Sunday.  Please bring a pair of gloves from home (or purchase at Go Ape!) for trooptop adventure</p>
<p style="text-align: left;">Treetop Adventure course will keep you challenged, present and living in the moment. So much more than just ziplines; it’s a 2-3-hour aerial journey through the forest canopy. Engage every sense in a fully immersive and visually stunning forest setting.</p>
<ul>
<li>Your adventure starts with a safety brief where we get you fitted into a harness and our instructors will help you learn the ropes.</li>
<li>Each site starts with a rope ladder or stairs up into the trees. Connect up and get climbing!</li>
<li>You’ll navigate wobbly bridges, traverse tricky crossings and, if you dare, conquer our unique free drop Tarzan swings*.</li>
<li>Ride our epic ziplines back down to the forest floor. Repeat.</li>
</ul>
