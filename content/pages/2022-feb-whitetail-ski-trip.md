<p><strong>Time: </strong>Meet at the St. Francis parking lot <b>at </b><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="color: #000000;"><b>6:00 PM Friday, February 18</b></span></span><b>,</b> returning late afternoon on Sunday, February 20th.  (sample 2020 January <a title="Whitetail Ski Trip Daily Schedule - Jan 2020" href="/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/WhitetailSkiTrip-DailySchedule-Jan2020.pdf">Ski Trip Daily Schedule</a>)</p>
<p>Scouts will enjoy skiing/snowboarding at Whitetail Mountain resort in Mercersburg, PA. on Saturday and tubing on Sunday. The troop will spend Friday and Saturday night at Joy El Camp and Retreats in group cabins – about 25 min from Whitetail Resort.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="background-color: #ffff00;"><strong>Because of the limit slots at Whitetail during holiday weekend for lift ticket and lesson package, please submit your registration as soon as possible. First come first serve.</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="background-color: #ffff00;"><strong>The Troop 55 basic package:</strong></span> <strong><span style="color: #ff0000;">$180 ($170 for adult driving own car)</span><br />
</strong>Includes Friday and Saturday night lodging, breakfast, bag lunch and dinner on Saturday, breakfast and lunch on Sunday, evening snacks, and tubing lift ticket on Sunday. <span style="text-decoration: underline;">Scouts must select their ski/snowboard options from the choices below</span>. Ski or snowboard equipment is available for rent, and rental rates include the price of the ski lift ticket. More information about what to bring will be explained at the Troop meeting.</p>
<p>Joy El lodging includes: basketball court &amp; game room (foosball, table tennis, air hockey, fireplace)</p>
<p><strong>If scouts plan on staying for night skiing Saturday evening, they should bring cash for dinner on Saturday. </strong> Scouts not interested in night skiing will be transported back to Joy El and dinner will be provided.</p>
<p><strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="background-color: #ffff00;">Ski/Snowboard Options</span> (Scouts can ski/snowboard 9:30 a.m. to 10 p.m. Saturday)</span></strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;">:</span></p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #993300;">1) All Mountain lift ticket only</span> (have own equipment):</strong>  $84<br />
<strong><span style="color: #993300;">2) Ski or snowboard rental </span>*:</strong>  $55<br />
<strong><span style="color: #993300;">3) Group lesson package (2 hour lesson, lift ticket and equipment rental )</span>*:</strong> $150</p>
<p><strong>**</strong> <strong>Group Lesson Package</strong> &#8211; This package is designed for the skier or snowboarder who is comfortable on all terrain. Package includes a lift ticket that is valid on all open terrain, rental equipment (includes helmet), and a group lesson at your ability level.</p>
<p><strong>Note: BSA regulations require a helmet.</strong> If you are not renting and plan on bringing your own equipment, but do not have a helmet, you must purchase a helmet rental. Helmets are now included in the group EZ and group All mountain package.</p>
