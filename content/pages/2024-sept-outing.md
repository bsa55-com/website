<p><strong>11:30am September 1st </strong><strong>Biking</strong> <strong>Option: <span style="background-color: #ffff00;">Troop 55 will not provide bike for the scouts</span>. <span style="background-color: #ffff00;">Scouts need to <strong>bring your own bicycle and <span style="color: #ff0000;">hamlet</span> for biking</strong></span>. Scouts will need to bring a bag lunch to eat on the trail. Bring water for the bike ride and</strong> wear comfortable clothes and shoes on a ride. Scouts must also be prepared cold day when they bike. As always, water bottles, sunscreen and rain gear is always a good idea<strong>.  <span style="background-color: #ffff00;">Parents</span> : </strong><span style="background-color: #ffff00;">please transport the bike for your own scout(s) to the St. Francis Church at drop off around <strong>6:00pm on August 31 </strong>, and prepare to<strong> pick up the bike at the Great Falls Library at 8:30am on Monday.  </strong></span></p>
<p><strong>11:30am September 1st </strong><strong>Hiking Option</strong>: Troop 55 scouts will enjoy a daypack hike on the Billy Goat Trail <strong>section A</strong> scrambling over steep cliffs, climbing on angled rocks and boulders and enjoying beautiful views of the Potomac River.  The return of the circuit hike is along the C&amp;O Canal Towpath. Please wear hiking boots and bring a day pack for rain gears and snacks and water.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Each Scout needs to bring a daypack, two Nalgene water bottles, snacks for the trail hike. As always, they need to be prepared for wet, cold, and windy weather on the trail and in camp. Raingear, non-cotton clothing layers, <strong>extra socks</strong>, hats, gloves, and hiking boots are highly recommended. Scouts will plan and prepare Sunday&#8217;s breakfast , lunch and dinner at camp site as a patrol. During these potentially cold trips, always err on the side of caution and PACK EXTRA CLOTHES &amp; WARM SLEEPING GEAR.</p>
